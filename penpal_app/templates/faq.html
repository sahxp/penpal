Basics

A penpal is someone with whom you exchange letters or messages, typically with someone from a different country or culture. The goal is to learn about their lives, experiences, and perspectives, while sharing your own.

To create a profile, you typically need to provide some basic information about yourself, such as your name, age, location, and interests. You may also be asked to upload a photo or write a short bio.

It's important to be cautious when sharing personal information online, especially with people you don't know well. You should never share your full name, address, phone number, or financial information with someone you just met online.
